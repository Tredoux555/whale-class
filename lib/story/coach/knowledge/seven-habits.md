# The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People — Stephen Covey

**Begin with the end in mind (Habit 2).** Know your destination — your values, your vision — so each step heads the right way. Write the personal mission.

**Put first things first (Habit 3) — the time-management matrix.** Urgent vs important:
- Q1 urgent+important (crises) — handle but don't live here.
- **Q2 important, NOT urgent (planning, prevention, growth, relationships, health) — THIS is where effective people invest. Most neglected, highest leverage.**
- Q3 urgent, not important (interruptions) — minimize.
- Q4 neither — eliminate.

**"The main thing is to keep the main thing the main thing."** Schedule your priorities; don't prioritize your schedule.

**Be proactive (Habit 1).** Work inside your Circle of Influence; don't burn energy in the Circle of Concern. Respond by values, not impulse.

**Sharpen the saw (Habit 7).** Renew the four dimensions — physical, mental, social/emotional, spiritual. You can't saw effectively with a dull blade; self-renewal is the habit that makes all the others sustainable.
