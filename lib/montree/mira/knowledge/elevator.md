## 30-second

Montree is the AI Montessori classroom revolution. A teacher takes a photo of a child working. The system identifies the work, records the observation, tracks the child's progress, and writes the parent report. It lifts the administrative weight off teachers so they can return to what actually matters — the children, the classroom, the craft.

## 90-second

Montree is an end-to-end school management system built specifically for Montessori. Three audiences, one product:

**Teachers** photograph children at work. Our two-pass AI (Haiku then Sonnet) identifies which Montessori material is in the frame, attributes it to the child, and writes the observation note. Teachers go from 20-minutes-per-child of paperwork to nothing.

**Parents** receive a Weekly Wrap report that reads like the teacher wrote it themselves. Real, specific, dated. Twelve languages, native quality.

**Principals** see the whole school through one cockpit. Astra, their AI chief of staff, scans every parent thread, drafts every reply, prepares the dossier for every difficult meeting. The principal walks in prepared.
