# Facebook Reels Playbook (Montree page)

> Added Jun 12, 2026 from Tredoux's optimization handoff, derived from
> performance analysis of the last 6 Reels on the Montree Facebook page.
> **PRIORITY: for Facebook Reels these rules OVERRIDE the generic
> caption/hashtag formulas elsewhere in this knowledge base** (e.g. the
> 5+10+5 hashtag mix does NOT apply — long hashtag walls hurt on this page).

## Performance data the rules are based on

| Reel | Posted | Views | Avg watch | Caption |

## Key takeaways

- **Reach is the bottleneck, not content.** The page has ~7 followers, so the
  organic seed audience is tiny. The best-watched Reel (31s avg) got the
  fewest views. Distribution must come from seeding + shares, not waiting.
- **Timing matters.** Best reach came from ~9 AM and ~6 PM posts. 9 PM and
  overnight (6 AM) posts consistently underperformed.
- **Retention vs. reach tradeoff.** The most-viewed Reels lost people at 4s;
  the well-watched Reel barely got distributed. Goal = combine both.
- **Engagement is ~zero.** Almost no reactions/comments/shares. Shares are
  the strongest expansion signal — and there are none. Every Reel must
  actively ask for them.
- **Long captions hurt.** The 8+ hashtag wall on "Meet Astra" didn't help.

## Rules to apply to EVERY Facebook Reel

1. **Hook in 1–2 seconds** — lead with the problem or a surprising claim,
   no intro, no logo card, no "hey guys".
2. **Post timing:** mornings (8–10 AM) or early evening (5–7 PM) only.
   Never 9 PM or overnight.
3. **Captions:** short, open with a question, max 3–4 specific hashtags.
4. **Always end with an engagement CTA** (a question or "tag a teacher who
   needs this") to drive shares.
5. **Seed every post:** cross-post to Instagram, share to relevant
   Montessori groups, and add to Stories immediately after publishing.

## Output format for Reel requests

When given a Reel idea or transcript, return:

- **Hook** — the 1–2 second opening line
- **Caption** — short, question-led, 3–4 hashtags
- **Post time** — recommended day/slot (within the windows above)
- **CTA** — engagement prompt
- **Seeding checklist** — cross-post + groups + Stories
