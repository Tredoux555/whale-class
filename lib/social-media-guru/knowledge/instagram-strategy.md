# Instagram Strategy Guide (2026)

## Algorithm Priorities
Instagram's algorithm in 2026 prioritizes content in this order:

1. **Reels** (Highest Reach)
   - Pushed to non-followers via Explore page
   - 60-90 second Reels perform best
   - Trending audio gives 3-5x reach boost
   - Use native Instagram camera for best distribution

2. **Carousels** (High Engagement)
   - High saves/shares signal quality to algorithm
   - Educational content performs exceptionally well
   - 5-10 slides optimal (people swipe through completely)
   - First slide must hook, last slide = CTA

3. **Stories** (Direct Engagement)
   - 24-hour lifespan, high visibility to followers
   - Interactive stickers (polls, questions) boost engagement
   - Post 3-7 stories per day (not in bulk, spread throughout day)

4. **Static Posts** (Lowest Reach)
   - Still valuable for grid aesthetics
   - Use for announcements, quotes, testimonials

## Optimal Posting Times for Education/Teachers
**Best times** (highest teacher/parent activity):
- **Weekdays:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Morning:** 7:00-9:00 AM EST (before school/commute)
- **Evening:** 6:00-8:00 PM EST (after work, planning time)

**Avoid:**
- Weekends (teachers in personal mode)
- Friday afternoons (mental checkout)
- Monday mornings (too busy)

## Content Mix Formula (80/20 Rule)
- **80% Value** - Teaching, tips, behind-the-scenes, inspiration
- **20% Promotion** - Product features, CTAs, links

For Montree:
- Show classrooms using the app (real teachers)
- Quick tips on Montessori method
- Parent communication examples
- Progress tracking demonstrations

## Hashtag Strategy
**Total count:** 15-20 hashtags (Instagram allows 30, but 15-20 is optimal)

**Mix formula:**
- 5 **Large** hashtags (100K+ posts) - for initial reach
- 10 **Medium** hashtags (10K-100K posts) - sweet spot for visibility
- 5 **Small** hashtags (under 10K posts) - easier to rank, niche audience

**Montessori-specific hashtags:**


## Reel Best Practices
**First 3 Seconds = Everything**
- Hook must be irresistible
- Text overlay on first frame (don't rely on caption)
- Show the transformation/result upfront

**Captions ON** (80% watch without sound)
- Auto-captions via Instagram or manual
- Keep text large, readable, contrasting background

**Length Sweet Spot:**
- 7-15 seconds for quick tips
- 30-60 seconds for tutorials
- 60-90 seconds for storytelling

**Trending Audio vs Original:**
- Trending audio = 3-5x reach (use when relevant)
- Original audio = lower reach but builds brand voice
- Mix: 70% trending, 30% original

**Engagement Triggers:**
- Ask a question in the caption
- Use "Tag a teacher who needs this"
- "Save this for later" (saves = high quality signal)

## Growth Tactics
**Collaboration:**
- Reels with other Montessori educators (cross-pollination)
- Guest takeovers (story swaps)
- Shoutouts in Stories

**Consistency:**
- Post 3-5 Reels per week minimum
- 3-7 Stories per day
- 1-2 feed posts per week

**Engagement Back:**
- Reply to every comment in first hour (boosts algorithm)
- Engage with followers' content (they'll reciprocate)
- Host Q&A sessions via Stories

## Red Flags (Algorithm Penalties)
- Reposting TikToks with watermark (use original)
- Engagement bait ("Comment YES for tips") - banned
- Too many hashtags (over 30)
- Asking for follows/likes directly in caption
- Low-quality/blurry videos
