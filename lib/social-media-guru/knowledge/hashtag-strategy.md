# Hashtag Strategy Guide

## The 80/20 Rule for Hashtags

**Use 15-20 hashtags per post** (Instagram allows 30, but 15-20 performs better)

**Mix formula:**
- 5 **Large** hashtags (100K+ posts) - for initial reach
- 10 **Medium** hashtags (10K-100K posts) - sweet spot for visibility
- 5 **Small** hashtags (under 10K posts) - easier to rank, niche audience

**Why this works:**
- Large hashtags: Your post appears in feeds briefly, gets quick views
- Medium hashtags: Best ROI - less competition, more engaged audience
- Small hashtags: You can dominate the feed, connect with true niche

## Platform-Specific Hashtag Rules

### Instagram
- **Placement:** Caption OR first comment (both work equally)
- **Optimal count:** 15-20 hashtags
- **Strategy:** Mix formula (5 large, 10 medium, 5 small)
- **Banned hashtags:** Check Instagram's banned list (changes frequently)

### TikTok
- **Placement:** Caption only
- **Optimal count:** 3-5 hashtags MAX
- **Strategy:** 2 trending + 1 niche
- **Format:** Integrate naturally into caption text

**TikTok example:**
```
POV: You're a Montessori teacher who just found a free classroom management app 😍

Game changer for progress tracking → link in bio

#montessoriteacher #teacherlife #teachertok
```

### Facebook
- **Groups:** NO HASHTAGS (triggers autocomplete dropdown bug)
- **Pages:** 3-5 hashtags max
- **Strategy:** Use sparingly, focus on value

### LinkedIn
- **Optimal count:** 3-5 hashtags
- **Strategy:** Professional keywords only
- **Placement:** End of caption

**LinkedIn example:**
```
After 5 years teaching Montessori, I built Montree to solve the admin time problem.

Try it: montree.xyz

#EdTech #MontessoriEducation #TeacherTools
```

## Common Hashtag Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Using the same 30 hashtags on every post**
   - Instagram flags this as spam
   - Rotate 3-5 hashtag sets

2. **All large hashtags**
   - Your post gets buried instantly
   - Mix sizes for sustainable reach

3. **Irrelevant hashtags**
   - #love #instagood #photooftheday
   - These attract bots, not teachers

4. **Hashtag stuffing in caption**
   - Makes caption unreadable
   - Put hashtags in first comment OR at the end after line breaks

5. **Not checking for banned hashtags**
   - Instagram shadowbans your post
   - Check each hashtag before using

6. **Using hashtags on Facebook groups**
   - Triggers autocomplete dropdown
   - Dismisses the entire post dialog
   - NEVER use hashtags in group posts
