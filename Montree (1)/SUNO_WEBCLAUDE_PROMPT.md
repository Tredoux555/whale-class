# Kickoff prompt — Suno songs (paste into Claude in Chrome)

> Use the **Claude in Chrome** extension (it can control the browser). Plain claude.ai can't operate Suno. Paste everything in the box below as your first message, then log in to Suno when it asks.

---

You're helping me mass-produce children's phonics songs in Suno. All the song content lives on one public web page — you read it there and paste it into Suno, one song at a time, top to bottom. I (the human) handle login, credits, listening, and downloads.

**Source page:** open `https://montree.xyz/pink-phase-songs.html` in a tab and keep it open. It has 49 songs, Lesson 5 → Lesson 53. Each song shows a title in quotes, a **Suno accent** line, and a **Lyrics** block.

For each song you paste two things into Suno:
- **STYLE** = the base style prompt below + a space + that song's "Suno accent" sentence.
- **LYRICS** = only the song's Lyrics verse lines. Do NOT include the "Movement" note or the "Sound / Keyword / Words sung" header.

**Base style prompt (prepend to every song's accent):**
"Bright, warm children's singalong for a kindergarten circle. Gentle acoustic ukulele or soft nylon-string guitar, light hand percussion — claps, shaker, a woodblock. One clear, friendly, unhurried voice. Nursery-rhyme simple: a short, easy melody a four-year-old can sing back after one listen. Very repetitive. Slow enough that every word is crisp. No competing backing vocals, no busy production."

**Suno steps, per song:**
1. Go to suno.com and open the Create page.
2. Turn ON **Custom** mode. Make sure **Instrumental is OFF** (we want sung vocals).
3. Paste **LYRICS** into the Lyrics box.
4. Paste **STYLE** into the Style / "Style of Music" box.
5. Put the song name in the Title field, formatted `Lesson 05 — The Snake Says Ssss`.
6. Click **Create**.
7. Then STOP and tell me: "Lesson N is generating — listen, keep the better take, and download it." Wait for me to say "next."

**Work order:** strictly top to bottom — Lesson 5, then 6, 7 … through 53. After each song, tell me which lesson you just did and which is next, so we can track progress.

**Stop and hand to me when:**
- Suno needs a login, email verification, or shows a captcha.
- Suno is out of credits or shows a paywall.
- After every Create — I listen and choose/download (don't guess my taste).
- Anything on screen differs from these steps — ask rather than guess.

**Rules:** keep STYLE identical for every song except the accent line (that consistency makes them feel like one songbook). Never invent lyrics or styles — use only what's on the source page.

Start now: open both tabs (the song page + Suno), confirm out loud that you can see Lesson 5's lyrics, then ask me to log in to Suno.
