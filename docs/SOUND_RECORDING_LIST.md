## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Quiet room** - no echo, no background noise
2. **Consistent distance** from mic
3. **Child-friendly pace** - not too fast
4. **Pure sounds** - no "uh" added to consonants
5. **Save as MP3** - 128kbps is fine
6. **File naming** - lowercase, no spaces
