**What makes Montree different?**

Montree is the first Montessori tracking and assessment system built around AI from the ground up. And as AI grows, so does Montree — it's quickly becoming a complete school management system.

Here's what that means for you.

**For teachers** — no more writing observations. Take a photo. Montree identifies the work and records it, and notes can be added anytime. With every observation it builds a real profile of each child: where they are in the Montessori sequence, and how they're growing as a person.

Behind it is an assistant trained on all of Maria Montessori's original writing and the most influential works in child development. It studies each child's progress and guides the teacher the way Maria herself might have. The result is a teacher with more time for what matters most — preparation, the classroom, the children.

**For parents** — Montree writes a weekly report for every child. Beautifully composed, with the week's photos woven in. A report that once took a teacher 45 minutes is ready in moments — and reads as though Montree watched the week unfold beside you.

**For the principal** — a calm, clear view of the whole school. Every classroom, every teacher, every student, every parent. Unmatched support, and oversight of every operation in one place.

Teachers, parents and the principal are all connected through Montree — every message in one place, with the full thread of every conversation. Nothing gets lost, and nothing slips through the cracks.

Montree is built for one purpose: to help. The teachers, the parents, the principal — and most of all the children, who get the most authentic Montessori experience possible.

That's the magic of Montree.

It isn't raising the bar. It's redrawing it.
