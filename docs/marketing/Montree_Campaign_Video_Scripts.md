# Montree — Campaign Video Production (Colossyan)

The standing system for every campaign video. Built for Colossyan Creator
(app.colossyan.com), driven by a browser-Claude. 13 videos: 1 front-page hero
+ 12 feature videos. Every video is a **talking-head** — one avatar speaking
to camera, on a clean background, with on-screen text for the key lines. No
B-roll, no stock footage, no screen recordings.

---

## 1. The standing workflow

1. A browser-Claude opens Colossyan and builds the video from this doc.
2. Colossyan **auto-splits the pasted narration into scenes** — one paragraph
   becomes one scene. Every script here is written so each paragraph IS one
   clean scene.
3. The browser-Claude enriches each scene with its on-screen text caption.
4. Preview → check the first 3 seconds → Generate.
5. Export natively per platform — never upload the identical file to TikTok,
   Reels and Shorts (all three suppress recycled video).

The script is the whole game. Colossyan executes a strong script with
precision; these 13 scripts are the blueprint.

---

## 2. Browser-Claude brief — paste this with any video

> You are building a Montree marketing video in Colossyan Creator
> (app.colossyan.com). It is a **talking-head explainer** — one avatar speaks
> to camera the whole way through. Tone: warm, calm, confident, premium —
> never hyped or salesy.
>
> **Setup**
> - If the project has leftover placeholder content (default filler script,
>   stray text boxes, a "Logo" placeholder), **clear it first** — start clean.
> - Aspect ratio: **16:9** for the hero; **9:16 vertical** for feature videos.
> - Avatar + voice: use the **default avatar** with the **"GB - Riley"** voice.
>   Use the same avatar and voice for every Montree video — consistency is the
>   brand.
> - **No B-roll, no stock footage, no screen recordings.** The avatar speaking
>   is the visual.
> - Background: one **clean, calm, consistent background** for the whole video
>   — a soft neutral or gentle green tint. Do not change it between scenes.
> - Music: soft and low, or none. It must never compete with the voice.
>
> **Build**
> - Paste the NARRATION block into the script editor. Colossyan auto-splits it
>   into scenes — one paragraph = one scene.
> - For each scene, add a **Text** element with the exact on-screen caption
>   from the video's table. Large, high-contrast, clear of the top/bottom safe
>   zones — readable with the sound off.
> - Enable subtitles / closed captions if available.
> - Keep transitions simple — a soft cut, or none.
>
> **Finish**
> - Preview. Watch the first 3 seconds hard — the opening line and its text
>   must land instantly.
> - Generate the video.
> - **If generating or downloading requires signing up or a paid plan: stop
>   and report back. Do not create an account or enter payment details.**
> - For social, export/post natively per platform — never reuse one file.

---

## 3. Copywriting principles (baked into every script below)

- First 3 seconds decide 80% of completion. The hook is short and layered —
  the spoken line + its on-screen text together.
- Pace ~150 words per minute. Hero ≈ 65s. Feature videos ≈ 22-26s.
- Structure: Hook → Problem → Solution → Proof → Action/Share.
- Feature videos end on a **share trigger** ("Send this to a teacher who…").
  The hero ends on the site CTA.
- No logos or intros at the start — open straight on value.
