### Shopping Strategy

1. **Sets first** — insect sets (spider, snail, crab, grasshopper), animal buckets (snake, duck, dragon, seal, swan), resin food sets (grapes, plum, pea)
2. **Same shop** — find shops selling 80+ miniature varieties at ¥0.30-1.00 each, buy 10+ from one shop to save shipping
3. **Hard-to-find items** — king (use chess piece), drum (search instrument sets), brick (search sandbox/building models)
