## Who we are

Montree ("Montree", "we", "us") provides software that helps Montessori and preschool **schools** manage classrooms, record observations, track children's progress, and communicate with parents. Our users are **teachers, principals, and parents** — not children. Children do not create accounts or log in to Montree.

For data that schools enter about their students, the **school is the data controller** and Montree acts as a **data processor** on the school's behalf, under our agreement with the school.

## Information we collect

**Account information (adults):** name, email address, password (stored hashed), role (teacher/principal/parent), and school affiliation.

**Information schools enter about children:** a child's name or nickname, classroom, age/age-group, photos of the child's work (and sometimes the child), observations, progress records, and reports. This information is entered by teachers and principals — never by children.

**Communications:** messages between teachers and parents, voice notes, and meeting summaries created within the app.

**Usage and device data:** basic logs needed to operate and secure the service (e.g., error logs, IP address, device/browser type). We do **not** track you across other companies' apps or websites, and we do **not** use third-party advertising.

## How we use information

To provide and operate Montree (record observations, generate reports, enable parent communication), to keep the service secure, to provide support, to process the school's subscription, and to improve the product. AI features (e.g., generating report drafts or identifying work in a photo) process the relevant content to produce that result.

## Children's privacy

Montree is designed for use by adults in a school setting. We do not knowingly allow children to create accounts or provide information directly. Children's information is provided by the school under its authority and, where required, with parental consent obtained by the school. We handle children's information only to provide the service to the school, never to advertise to or profile children. Parents may contact their school, or us, to review or request deletion of their child's information.

## How we share information

We share information only with service providers who help us run Montree, under contracts that require them to protect it:

- **Supabase** — database and file storage
- **Stripe** — subscription billing
- **Anthropic** and **OpenAI** — AI features (report drafting, photo insight)
- **Resend** — transactional email
- **Agora** — voice/video features (where used)

We do not sell personal information. We do not share children's personal information with third parties for their own purposes.

## Data retention and deletion

We keep information for as long as the school's account is active or as needed to provide the service. **You can delete your account and associated personal data from within the app** (Settings → Delete account), or by contacting us. Schools can request deletion of student data. We delete or anonymize data we are not legally required to keep.

## Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us (below) and we'll respond as required by law. For student data, requests are handled with the school as controller.

## Security

We protect data with encryption in transit, access controls, row-level database security, and least-privilege access. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to notify affected parties of incidents as required by law.

## Changes

We'll update this policy as needed and revise the "last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated to schools.
