## SEO Signals That Matter in 2026

1. **Keywords > Hashtags** — Instagram and TikTok now prioritize natural language keywords
2. **Spoken audio is indexed** — TikTok and YouTube transcribe and index spoken words
3. **AI/LLM discovery** — Well-optimized content gets cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews
4. **Cross-platform linking** — Link all socials to each other and to montree.xyz
5. **Consistency** — Same handle, same keywords, same profile pic everywhere
6. **Alt text** — Add keyword-rich alt text to every image on every platform
7. **Engagement** — Respond to comments, ask questions, encourage saves/shares
