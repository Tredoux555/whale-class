### Making Sandpaper Letters (if buying not possible)

**Materials:**
- MDF board (3mm thick) - Â¥30
- Fine sandpaper (180 grit) - Â¥15
- Letter stencils - Â¥20 or print
- Wood glue - Â¥10

**Steps:**

4. Cut sandpaper letters carefully

## âš ï¸ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Teaching letter NAMES before SOUNDS**
   - Always say /m/ not "em"
   - Sound-first builds phonemic awareness

2. **Skipping Sound Games**
   - I-Spy is the foundation
   - Don't rush to letters

3. **Too many letters at once**
   - Introduce 3-4 per week maximum
   - Mastery before moving on

4. **Uppercase before lowercase**
   - Children read books in lowercase
   - Uppercase is extension, not introduction

5. **Random letter order**
   - Use phonetic groupings (c, m, a, t first)
   - Allows word building quickly

6. **No control of error**
   - Always have picture/control cards
   - Child should self-check
