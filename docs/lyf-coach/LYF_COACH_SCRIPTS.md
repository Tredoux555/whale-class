## 2. The explainer — 60s (Sealed Individual)
**Visual:** real screen — journal a day, get a reflection, plan tomorrow with rest built in.
> Most apps that "listen" are really just collecting.
> Lyf Coach is the opposite. It's a coach, a journal, and a planner in one — and it's sealed.
> What you say stays yours. We can't read it. That's not a setting; it's how it's built.
>
> Talk to it like a friend who has your back. It reflects what you're carrying, guards you
> from taking on too much, protects your sleep, and ends every day with one clear next step.
> No streaks to chase. No data to sell. No one over your shoulder.
>
> It's not a therapist and it won't pretend to be — if something's heavy, it'll gently point
> you to a real person who can help. The rest of the time, it's just… yours.
>
> **Lyf Coach. Work smarter, not harder — and finally, privately.**

## 3. The founder promise — 45s (Tredoux, real face/voice)
**Visual:** Tredoux, plain, talking to camera.
> I built this because I wanted somewhere to think that wasn't being harvested.

> If a kid ever uses the family version and says something frightening, even then it stays
