# LEVEL 2 — The Pattern Year begins (W27–42) — DEVELOPING

**Focus arc: digraphs (6 wks) → blends (5 light wks) → magic-e (4 wks) → soft c/g + tch/dge celebration (1 wk).**
Blends are consolidation (recombining known sounds), not new sound-symbol knowledge — hence the lighter,
faster treatment with heavy picture-vocab. Digraphs and magic-e are genuine new content at Level 1's
one-pattern-per-week cadence. EAL (Mandarin-L1) adjustments are structural, not decorative: th minimal
pairs W29, ng explicit W31, final blends split+doubled W36–37, final-l trap W37.

**Snake W33** (name-exempt; spellable W38 🎉). Potato: still never printed — but with magic-e the class can
*nearly* spell it, and they know it.
