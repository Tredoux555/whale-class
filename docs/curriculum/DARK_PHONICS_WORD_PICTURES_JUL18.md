## Notes on word selection
- Skipped: pure onomatopoeia/sound-stutters (ssss, mmm, zzz, vroom, tick-tock, ha-ha), function words, standalone abstract adjectives with no single clear visual on their own (hot, wet, fun, big, yum, yes), and bare numbers (six, ten) — their concrete nouns are already pictured (e.g. "ten hens" -> the hen picture).
- Reused characters across lessons (goat: L10+L13 · dog: L12+L14 · fox: L23+L28 · sock: L07+L17) each reference THEIR OWN lesson's image (e.g. L13's goat prompt says `lesson-13.png`, not L10's), since each lesson has its own distinct source illustration even though the subject repeats.
- L25 (jam/jar), L26 (van/vet), L29 (yo-yo/yam) have no animal character — the lesson's *object* subject (jam, van, yo-yo) is still flagged as the character/style reference word for visual continuity with that lesson's deck image.
