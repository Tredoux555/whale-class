Part 3
The 60-Second Pitch Script
Use this when presenting the deck live, recording a voiceover, or talking to a principal. Timed to the 3 scenes.
Scene 1 — The Pain (0:00 – 0:20)
"You know that massive ledger accountants used before QuickBooks? Rows and columns, hours of tedious work, easy to make mistakes? That's Montessori record-keeping today. Every child, every work, every observation — tracked by hand. 24 children, 213 curriculum works, over five thousand data points a year. Teachers are losing 8 hours a week to paperwork that should take minutes."
📌 Let the scrolling ledger and ticking clock do the heavy lifting. Pause. Let them feel it.
Scene 2 — The Solution (0:20 – 0:45)
"Montree changes that. One tap to track a child's work. The entire Montessori curriculum — mapped and ready. Progress bars update in real-time. Parent reports generate automatically. No more late nights filling in forms. No more wondering which child needs what next — Montree's Guru tells you. Built by a Montessori teacher, for Montessori teachers."
📌 Click to Scene 2. Point at the dashboard. Let the progress bars animate. The visual contrast does the selling.
Scene 3 — The Offer (0:45 – 1:00)
"We call this the QuickBooks moment for Montessori. And right now, we're offering a free 3-month pilot to schools who want to be first. No credit card. No risk. Just modern tools for the classroom that deserves them. montree.xyz"
📌 Click to Scene 3. Let "The QuickBooks moment for Montessori" land. End on the URL. Silence is powerful.

Part 5
4-Week Launch Sprint
Practical actions, week by week. You're teaching full-time, so everything is designed for 30 minutes a day max.
Week 1 — Arm the Weapons
Create all content from the pitch deck
Turn the pitch into every format you need.
  • Screen-record the 3-scene pitch with voiceover (use the script above)
  • Cut a 15-second "Ledger Problem" clip from Scene 1
  • Screenshot the before/after for carousel posts
  • Embed the pitch animation as the hero section of montree.xyz
  • Write the WeChat article version with screenshots
  • Prepare the principal cold email template
Week 2 — Seed the Groups
Plant the "Ledger Problem" in Montessori communities
Don't sell. Start conversations.
  • Join 5 Montessori Facebook groups (don't post yet, engage first)
  • Post the "Ledger Problem" hook video in groups that allow it
  • Share the Before/After carousel on Instagram + 小红书
  • Post WeChat article on your 公众号
  • DM 5 Montessori teachers you know personally — offer free pilot
  • Share in any Beijing international school WhatsApp/WeChat groups
Week 3 — Activate Pilots
Get 3–5 schools using Montree for free
Onboard them personally. Their experience IS your testimonial.
  • Help each pilot school through signup (be their concierge)
  • Collect first-week feedback — fix bugs immediately
  • Ask pilot teachers: "What would you show a parent to prove this works?"
  • Screenshot real dashboards (with permission) for future content
  • Post a "Day 1" update in Facebook groups: "Started piloting with 3 schools..."
Week 4 — Build Proof
Turn pilot data into case studies
Real numbers. Real teachers. Real before/after.
  • Calculate time saved per teacher per week (target: the "8 hours" claim)
  • Get 1-2 video testimonials from pilot teachers (even 30 seconds)
  • Write a case study: "[School Name] reduced record-keeping from 8hrs to 30min"
  • Update the pitch deck Scene 2 with real data, not mockup data
  • Begin cold-emailing principals with the case study attached
  • Post the full 60-second pitch video to YouTube and LinkedIn

⚠️ Privacy-First Approach

You can't use student footage, and you shouldn't. All content uses the pitch animation (no real children), mockup dashboards, and anonymized data. When you get pilot schools, ask permission for anonymized case studies only. Your school won't appear in any content. The pitch deck IS the content — that's the whole point.
