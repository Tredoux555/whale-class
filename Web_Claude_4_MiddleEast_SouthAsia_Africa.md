## WHAT TO SEARCH
For EACH country, search these sources:
- Google: "[Country/City] international Montessori school"
- Google: "[Country/City] AMI Montessori school"
- montessori-ami.org/find-a-school (filter by country)
- edarabia.com (EXCELLENT for Middle East schools — filter by Montessori)
- For UAE specifically: also search khda.gov.ae (Dubai school ratings), adek.gov.ae (Abu Dhabi), whichschooladvisor.com
- For India: also search montessoriindia.org (Indian Montessori Foundation — publishes principal contacts), montessori-ami.org India listings
- For Kenya: search montessori.or.ke (Montessori Kenya)
- For Morocco: search in French "école Montessori [ville]"

## WHAT TO COLLECT FOR EACH SCHOOL
Put the results in a simple table with these columns:
| SchoolName | Email | Country | City | Website | ContactPerson | Accreditation | AgeRange | Notes |

3. **Gulf state international schools** — Qatar, Saudi, Bahrain, Oman, Kuwait all have growing international school sectors
4. **AMI accredited anywhere** — always top priority regardless of country
5. **Kenya and Morocco** — smaller markets but growing, especially Nairobi
