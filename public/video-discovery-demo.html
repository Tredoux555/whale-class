Whale Platform - YouTube Video Automation System

🚀 Quick Start

Click the button below to trigger video discovery for your Montessori curriculum works.

📖 How It Works

  1. Search: System searches YouTube for each curriculum work
  2. Score: Each video is scored 0-100 based on relevance
  3. Save: Best videos (score ≥ 60) are saved to database
  4. Approve: You review and approve videos in admin dashboard
  5. Display: Approved videos appear automatically on curriculum pages

🎯 Expected Results

  • 52-59 videos found (70-80% coverage)
  • Average score: 75-85/100
  • Duration: 2-3 minutes
  • Cost: $0 (within free tier)
