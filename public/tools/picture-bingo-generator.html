Picture Bingo Generator

Real photos on front, words on back — duplex print ready

How to play: 1) Print the Bingo Boards (duplex — flip on short edge) — one per player.
2) Print the Calling Cards — cut along the dashed lines to make individual cards.
3) The caller draws a card, shows the picture, says the word. Players cover the matching picture on their board.
Click Generate Bingo Boards for player boards, or Generate Calling Cards for cut-out tabs.
