Which sheets are which book?
After printing all 32 sheets, separate them in order:
1–5 Snake in My Sock (s) · 6–10 An Apple for Ant (a) · 11–15 The ___ Sat (t) · 16–21 SPAT! (p) · 22–27 Sit! Sit! Sit! (i) · 28–32 Nap, Ant, Nap (n). Fold & staple each stack.
