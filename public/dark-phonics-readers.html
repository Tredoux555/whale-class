Dark Phonics - Easy Readers

Tightened decodable readers - 5 pages, funny, every word legal at its sound-gate. The kid reads it alone.

How these work: each reader sits at a sound-gate and uses ONLY letters taught up to that point (checked by script - no cheating). Short, silly, 5 pages: pick up, read, close, feel like a hero. Each is linked to the song(s) at that gate. Picture prompts are deliberately wild (a tower of cats, six foxes in one box) for vocab + giggles - paste into Midjourney (shared --sref 9999; use the cover character as --cref).
