What makes us different

Five things no other system can do

These aren't features. They're fundamental capabilities that require a completely different architecture.

01
Visual Work Recognition
The camera sees a child with Montessori materials and identifies the specific work — "Sandpaper Letters," "Golden Beads," "Pouring Water." No barcodes. No QR codes. No manual selection from a dropdown. The AI sees what the teacher sees.
02
A Brain That Gets Smarter Every Day
Every teacher correction teaches the system. "That's not Cutting — that's Sewing." Now it knows. The more your teachers use it, the more accurate it becomes. Your classroom builds its own visual memory. No other system learns from its mistakes.
03
Parent Reports That Are Actually Personal
Not "Your child had a great week!" Not a template with a name swapped in. A genuine letter explaining what your child worked on, what they're developing, what comes next, and why it matters — written from the photos the teacher took that week.
04
The Smart Shelf
For every child, every week: five works across five curriculum areas, chosen by AI based on what they've mastered, what they're ready for, and which sensitive periods are active. The teacher opens the app and sees exactly what to present next. Like GPS for Montessori progression.
05
A Montessori Expert on Demand
A parent asks: "Why is my child doing the same work every day?" Instead of a 20-minute email, Montree answers instantly — with developmental context, sensitive period awareness, and specific reference to their child's activity. Teachers get the same expert guidance, voice-first, right on the child's page.
