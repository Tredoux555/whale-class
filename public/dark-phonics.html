Dark Phonics

49 slow, call-and-response trap songs - one silly catch phrase per sound. Built for young English-learners.

Per song: the yellow line is the catch phrase the kids shout back. In Suno (Custom mode, Instrumental OFF): paste Style into the style box, Lyrics into the lyrics box, Title into the title. Slow + repetitive on purpose - leave the gaps so the class copies. Tap copy on any box.
Lesson 5 Lesson 5 · s — /s/ — "The Snake Says Ssss"
“snake in my sock!”
Title 
Lesson 05 - s - Dark Phonics
Style 
Slow, gentle dark trap for young English-learners - calm and playful, never aggressive. Soft 808 bass, lazy trap hi-hats, minor key, simple hypnotic bell melody, slow around 68 BPM with lots of space. Call-and-response: one clear, unhurried lead voice; a small group of children repeat each line back. Very simple English, very repetitive. Leave a clear gap after every line so the children can copy it. One silly catch phrase, repeated often. Bare consonant cues written in (brackets) are whispered and percussive - no vowel, never 'uh'.
