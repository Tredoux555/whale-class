How to use Dark Phonics

One silly song per sound, in teaching order (lessons 5–53). Same path as the weekly curriculum.

🎵 Songs — play this week's song every day. Repetition is the whole trick.

🃏 Flashcards — after singing, flash the cards: the scene first ("snake in my sock!"), then each word. Print two-sided with normal settings — fronts and backs line up by themselves.

📕 Books & Easy Readers — the child reads these ALONE. "Gate: Lesson N" means: hand it out once the class has passed that song.

🎤 Sing-Along Cards — lyric cards for circle time.

Daily rhythm (10 min): sing → flash the cards → done.
