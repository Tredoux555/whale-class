Why bundled
-----------
mvgen embeds the theme font into ffmpeg via `fontsdir` so the burned-in
subtitles render identically on any machine. Without bundling, macOS silently
substitutes a different serif for the montree theme's Lora (the CI/sandbox
happened to have Lora installed; the user's Mac does not), changing the look.
