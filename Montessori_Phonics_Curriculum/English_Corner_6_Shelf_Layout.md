# Montessori English Corner: 6-Shelf Layout
## For Ages 3-5 | From Consonants to Stories

---

## Overview

This layout takes children from letter sounds to reading fluency in a logical, Montessori-aligned progression. Each shelf represents approximately 2-4 months of work for most children.

**Total Works: ~45 works across 6 shelves**
**Progression Time: 18-24 months (varies by child)**

---

## SHELF 1: Sound Foundations
*"I hear the sounds"*

| Position | Work | Materials |

**Story Themes (from our curriculum):**
1. "The Big Red Hat" (CVC focus)
2. "Chip and the Fish" (Digraphs focus)
3. "Frog on a Log" (Blends focus)
4. "The Bee in the Tree" (Vowel teams focus)

**Comprehension Activities:**
- Who/What/Where questions
- Sequence ordering
- Picture-to-text matching
- Retelling cards

## Daily Rotation Suggestions

### Morning Work Cycle (2.5-3 hours)
Children choose freely from available shelves based on their level:

| Child Level | Primary Shelf | Can Also Access |
|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Beginner | Shelf 1 | - |
| Early Reader | Shelf 2-3 | Shelf 1 |
| Developing | Shelf 4-5 | Shelves 1-3 |
| Fluent | Shelf 6 | All shelves |

### Teacher Guidance
- Give individual or small-group lessons on new works
- Observe which shelf child gravitates toward
- Progress when child shows mastery (not boredom)

## What Comes Next? (Age 5-6+)

For children who complete all 6 shelves, add a **Challenge Shelf** with:

1. **Diphthongs**
   - oi/oy (oil, boy)
   - ou/ow (out, cow)
   - au/aw (haul, paw)

2. **R-Controlled Vowels**
   - ar (car, star)
   - or (for, more)
   - er/ir/ur (her, bird, turn)

3. **Complex Patterns**
   - ck, kn, wr, ph, wh
   - spl, str, scr, spr
   - -tion, -sion endings

4. **Chapter Books**
   - Early chapter book baskets
   - Book reports
   - Reading journals

## Tips for Success

1. **Follow the child** - Some zip through, others need repetition
2. **Don't skip Shelf 1** - Strong phonemic awareness = faster progress
3. **Overlap is okay** - Child can work on Shelf 3 while reviewing Shelf 2
4. **Make it beautiful** - Well-organized, inviting shelves draw children in
5. **Read aloud daily** - Comprehension develops through listening too
6. **Celebrate milestones** - First CVC word, first book, etc.
