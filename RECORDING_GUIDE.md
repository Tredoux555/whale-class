## RECORDING TIPS
- **Length**: Each sound = 1-2 seconds max
- **Distance**: Phone 6 inches from mouth
- **Environment**: No AC humming, no outside noise
- **Voice**: Clear, natural, slightly exaggerated
- **Format**: Export as MP3

## THE 26 SOUNDS TO RECORD

### âœ… PHASE 1: Easy (8 sounds)
Record these pure sounds - NO "uh" at end!

| Sound | How to Say | Common Mistake | File Name |
|-------|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| /s/ | Hissing snake "sssss" | Adding "suh" | s.mp3 |
| /m/ | Humming "mmmm" | Adding "muh" | m.mp3 |
| /f/ | Blow candle "ffff" | Adding "fuh" | f.mp3 |
| /n/ | Nose hum "nnnn" | Adding "nuh" | n.mp3 |
| /p/ | Pop lips (no voice) | Adding "puh" | p.mp3 |
| /t/ | Tongue tap (no voice) | Adding "tuh" | t.mp3 |
| /c/ or /k/ | Back of throat click | Adding "kuh" | k.mp3 |
| /h/ | Breath out "hhhh" | Making it too strong | h.mp3 |

### âœ… PHASE 2: Medium (6 sounds)

| Sound | How to Say | Common Mistake | File Name |
|-------|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| /b/ | Lips pop + voice | Sounds like /p/ | b.mp3 |
| /d/ | Tongue tap + voice | Sounds like /t/ | d.mp3 |
| /g/ | Throat + voice | Sounds like /k/ | g.mp3 |
| /j/ | "dge" sound | Too much "juh" | j.mp3 |
| /w/ | Lips rounded "www" | Adding "wuh" | w.mp3 |
| /y/ | Start of "yes" | Adding "yuh" | y.mp3 |

### âœ… PHASE 3: ESL Challenge (9 sounds)
Chinese speakers struggle with these - record clearly!

| Sound | How to Say | ESL Note | File Name |
|-------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| /v/ | Teeth on bottom lip + voice | NOT /w/! Vibrate! | v.mp3 |
| /th/ | Tongue BETWEEN teeth | Doesn't exist in Chinese! | th.mp3 |
| /r/ | Tongue curled BACK | NOT /l/! Don't touch roof! | r.mp3 |
| /l/ | Tongue touches roof | NOT /r/! Must touch! | l.mp3 |
| /z/ | Buzzy /s/ | Voice it! Like bee! | z.mp3 |
| /sh/ | Lips rounded "shhhh" | Hold it out | sh.mp3 |
| /ch/ | Like /t/ + /sh/ | Choo choo! | ch.mp3 |
| /ks/ | Two sounds together | For letter X | ks.mp3 |
| /kw/ | Two sounds together | For letter Q | kw.mp3 |
