# Complete Montessori English Corner: 6 Shelves
## From Consonants to Free Reading by Age 5

---

## The Goal

**By age 5, children completing this English corner will be able to:**
- Read kindergarten-level books independently
- Decode words with any common spelling pattern
- Read simple chapter books with support
- Free-read in the classroom reading corner

**Philosophy:** All works are designed for independent use. The child controls their own learning journey.

## Independent Work Design

Every work is designed so children can:

1. **Self-select** - Choose based on interest and readiness
2. **Self-correct** - Control cards show answers
3. **Self-pace** - Repeat as needed
4. **Self-extend** - Harder versions available

### Control of Error Built Into Each Work

| Work Type | Self-Correction Method |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Three-part cards | Control card shows picture + word together |
| Word building | Picture on mat matches completed word |
| Sentence matching | Color-coded dots on back |
| Story sequencing | Numbers on back reveal order |
| Comprehension cards | Flip card for answer |

```
START: Age 3-3.5
    ↓
SHELF 1: Sound Foundations (2-4 months)

    ↓ Mastery: Digraphs + blends
    ↓
SHELF 4: Green Part 1 (2-3 months)

SHELF 5: Green Part 2 (2-3 months)

    ↓
SHELF 6: Fluency (ongoing)
    ↓
END: Age 5 → FREE READER
```

## Daily Schedule Suggestion

### Morning Work Cycle (2.5-3 hours)

Children freely choose from their current shelf level:

**Beginner (Shelf 1):**
- Teacher gives sandpaper letter lesson
- Child traces and practices independently
- Plays I-Spy with objects

**Emerging Reader (Shelf 2-3):**
- Child selects word family work
- Builds words with movable alphabet
- Reads Pink/Blue Series book independently

**Developing Reader (Shelf 4-5):**
- Child explores vowel patterns
- Sorts words by pattern
- Reads Green Series books

**Fluent Reader (Shelf 6):**
- Sentence building
- Story comprehension cards
- Free reading from kindergarten basket

### Evening at Home (15-20 min)
- Digital phonics game (matches current shelf)
- Parent reads aloud from take-home book
- Child re-reads familiar book to parent
